Welcome to robo i/o!

Robo i/o is a two day workshop for junior high schoolers hosted by OHI/O, Ohio State's hackathon program. OHI/O aims to foster a tech culture at Ohio State and its surrounding communities.

We aim to provide junior high school students an opportunity to learn about computer science in a fun, engaging, and inclusive environment. Students will work in teams to build projects with TI-Nspire calculators and innvator hubs. Students will also gain the opportunity to develop new friendships, build connections with professional mentors, and learn soft skills that will help them succeed in the real world.
